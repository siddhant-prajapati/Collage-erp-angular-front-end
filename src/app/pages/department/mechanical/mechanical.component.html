Mechanical Department

There are plenty of reasons to choose mechanical engineering, but 5 prominent reasons are highlighted here.

  1. Great career ahead: Mechanical engineers have good graduate prospects. In any field or factory, company related to even computer, civil, IT, Chemical, Electrical, Machines and Infrastructures are common which requires mechanical engineers. That’s why it is called ever green branch. There is an opportunity for mechanical engineers to create and build mechanical devices which we use every day (day to day life).
  2. Salary: Mechanical engineers enjoy great starting salaries, with condition that in any skill they gain expertise. People with mechanical engineering skills are highly valued in society and their salaries reflect that fact. Mechanical engineers are multi skilled and multifaceted.
  3. Government schemes for becoming an entrepreneur: “Make in India” and “Atmanirbhar Bharat” are few of the government initiatives which provides great opportunities to become an entrepreneur. Perhaps the biggest satisfaction as a Mechanical Engineer is being an owner of an industry. Rajkot is considered as the hub of manufacturing industry thereby proving Mechanical Engineering as worth career option.
  4. Design: Sketch or drawing of ideas (creative /innovative) on paper. Several software is used to create design: AutoCAD, Pro e, Solid works, NX etc. If you are interested in white collar job or inclined to software, then mechanical engineering is best option.
