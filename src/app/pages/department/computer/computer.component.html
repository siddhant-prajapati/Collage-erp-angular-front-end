Computer Department

Computer engineering is a field of study that integrates principles and practices of computer science and electrical engineering to develop hardware and software components of computing systems. It encompasses a broad range of topics including computer architecture, digital electronics, embedded systems, operating systems, software engineering, and network engineering.

    Vision

    • Mission

  • To enhance industry linkage & alumni network for better placement and real-world exposure.
  • To provide various opportunities & platforms for all round development of students & encourage them for value-based practices.
